What Makes us Different?

ISO Certified Processes Automated Tracking Customer Relations Corporate Partners

ISO 27001 Certified Processes

Data Security is of utmost importance in the present day as we conduct transactions with overseas embassies online. The systems we use to store confidential data and manage them ensure security and efficiency at all costs.

As an ISO 27001 Certified Visa Consultancy firm, we assure our clients of Data Security in all our processes.

Automated Tracking

To minimize manual processes that may incur faults, we have integrated an Automated Visa Application Tracking Tool into our Visa systems. This application assists our staff and clients with submission, collection and delivery reminders through SMS alerts and Emails.

Built using up-to-date technology, our Automated Tracking Tool makes us stand out in terms of satisfactory service delivery.

Dedicated Quality control and Customer Relations Team

Understanding our customer’s experience helps us serve them better in the future. Our dedicated QC team and Customer Experience Team is equipped with all information and customer journeys.

If in case any of our clients meet with an unpleasant situation or an inconvenient experience, our team is ready to listen and serve you no matter what time of the day it is.

The clientele of Renowned Corporate Partners

The Consultants at KVA have years of experience working with some of the top Visa firms, Consulates, Travel Agencies and Student Consultancy firms in the country.

Following our inception in 2019, over the past few years, we’ve gathered a clientele of reputed Corporate Partners in Sri Lanka and overseas, with whom we jointly offer top-notch and unmatched services for our clients.
